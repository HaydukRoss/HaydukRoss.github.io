How to Create an Avatar in HeyGen

Step 1: Open HeyGen
Go to HeyGen.com and log in.

Note: Free accounts have limited capabilities.

Step 2: Navigate to Avatar Creation
Click the Avatars tab in the main menu.
Step 3: Choose Your Avatar Type
Select a stock avatar or create a custom avatar.
Step 4: Customize Your Avatar
Adjust voice, language, and gestures. Preview before saving.
Step 5: Generate
Click Generate, then export for use in projects.
