How to Create an Avatar in HeyGen

By Ross Hayduk • A quick, visual job aid with best-practice tips

  1. Open HeyGen

    Go to HeyGen.com and log in. If you’re new, sign up for free to get started.

    info
    Note: free accounts have limited capabilities.

  2. Navigate to Avatar Creation

    Click the Avatars tab in the main menu to open the creation dashboard.

    flag
    Explore categories from UGC to studio-style characters.

  3. Choose Your Avatar Type

    Select a stock avatar or create a custom avatar. Start with Public Avatars if you’re experimenting.

    info
    Heads up: custom avatars may require an additional subscription.

  4. Customize Your Avatar

    Adjust voice, language, and gestures. Use lip-sync for scripts, then preview before saving.

    psychology
    Match the voice to the character to reduce the “uncanny valley” effect.

  5. Generate

    Click Generate to produce your avatar. Use it in HeyGen projects or export it.

    check_circle
    Saved: your avatar is ready for use.

Now, review the five steps

  1. Open HeyGen.
  2. Go to Avatars.
  3. Choose an avatar.
  4. Customize voice and gestures.
  5. Click Generate.

Tip: keep images in an images/ folder and name them step1.jpg, step2.jpg, etc. All images scale responsively.
