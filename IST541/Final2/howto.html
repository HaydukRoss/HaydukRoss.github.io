How to Create an Avatar in HeyGen

By Ross Hayduk

Step 1: Open HeyGen
Go to HeyGen.com and log in with your account. If you don't have one, sign up for FREE to get started.

flag Note: Free accounts have limited capabilities.

Step 2: Navigate to Avatar Creation
Once logged in, click the Avatars tab in the main menu. This opens the avatar creation dashboard.

flag Explore the different avatar categories, from UGC to studio-style characters.

Step 3: Choose Your Avatar Type
You can select a stock avatar or create a custom avatar. Start with Public Avatars.

flag Custom avatars may require an additional subscription.

Step 4: Customize Your Avatar
Adjust voice, language, and gestures. HeyGen supports lip-sync for scripts. Preview before saving.

flag Match the voice to the character to avoid the “uncanny valley” effect.

Step 5: Generate
Click Generate to produce your avatar. Use it in HeyGen projects or export it.

check_circle Saved: Your avatar is ready for use.

Now, Review the Five Steps
  1. Open HeyGen.
  2. Go to Avatars.
  3. Choose an avatar.
  4. Customize voice and gestures.
  5. Click Generate.

check_circle Great work! You’re on your way to mastering HeyGen.
