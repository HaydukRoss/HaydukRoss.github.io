Guided Notes

What are AI avatars?

AI avatars are synthetic presenters that can deliver micro-lectures and demos at scale with consistent quality.

When should I use them?

Use avatars for repeatable explanations, multilingual content, or when on-camera SMEs are unavailable.

Ethics and accessibility

Disclose synthetic media, provide captions/transcripts, and follow accessibility best practices.
